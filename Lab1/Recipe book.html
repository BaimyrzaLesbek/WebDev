Slow Cooker Butter Chicken

  • Time: 4 h 30 m
  • Serves: 6
Ingredients Quantity
Butter 2 tbs
Vegetable Oil 2 tbs
Skinless, boneless chicken thighs 4 thighs
Onion 1 onion
Garlic 3 cloves
Curry Powder 2 tsp
Curry Paste 1 tbs
Tandoori Masala 2 tsp
Garam Masala 1 tsp
Tomato Paste 1 can (6 oz)
Green Cardamom Pods 15 pods
Low Fat Plain Yogurt 1 cup
Coconut Milk 1 can (14 oz)

Step 1: Melt the butter and vegetable oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Stir in the chicken, onion, and garlic. Cook and stir until the onion has softened and turned translucent, about 10 minutes. Stir in the curry powder, curry paste, tandoori masala, garam masala, and tomato paste until no lumps of tomato paste remain. Pour into a slow cooker, and stir in the cardamom pods, coconut milk, and yogurt. Season to taste with salt.

Step 2: Cook on High 4 to 6 hours, or on Low 6 to 8 hours until the chicken is tender and the sauce has reduced to your desired consistency. Remove and discard the cardamom pods before serving.

English breakfast

  • Time: 35 min
  • Serves: 1
Ingredients Quantity
Sausage 2 medium
Tomato 1 (large)
Butter 2 tablespoons, devided
Hash browns Â¼ (16 ounce) package frozen, thawed
Fresh mushrooms 1 (6 ounce) package sliced
Can baked beans 1 (15 ounce)
Egg 1 large
Bread 2 slices
Salt and pepper for taste

Step 1: Preheat the oven to 220 degrees C.
Strep 2: Brown the sausages on all sides in a frying pan over medium heat for 5 minutes. Transfer to a baking dish. Bake the sausages in the preheated oven for 10 minutes.
Step 3: Score a cross into the bottom of the tomato and place, cross-side up, in the baking dish with the sausages. Bake the sausages and the tomato for 10 minutes. Turn off the oven, but do not remove the sausages and tomato.
Step 4: Meanwhile, in the frying pan used to brown the sausages, melt 1 tablespoon of butter and fry, hash browns, and the mushrooms over high heat until the mushrooms have softened, and the hash browns turn golden, about 7 minutes. Transfer to the oven to keep warm.
Step 5: Pour beans in a small saucepan and cook, stirring frequently, over medium heat until heated through.
Step 6: Crack egg into a skillet over medium heat. Cook until outer edges become opaque, about 1 minute. Cover, reduce heat to low, and cook until whites are completely set, about 4 minutes.
Step 7: Toast bread and spread remaining butter over.
Step 8: Bring together the sausages, hash browns, bacon, beans, tomato, mushrooms, fried egg, and toast on a warm serving plate. Season with salt and pepper, garnish with parsley, and serve immediately.
